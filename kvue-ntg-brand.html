BRAND & IDENTITY

Scaling Neutrogena's distinctive brand assets

YEAR – 2023
ROLE – Brand Experience Designer @ Kenvue
STATUS – Launched in 2023, new phase on-going.

Neutrogena® is the #1 dermatologist recommended brand in the US and has been a longtime consumer favorite. However, the brand has been losing relevance among young consumers. In order to future-proof the branding system, the team launched a new point of difference, 'Boost Your Skin's Vitals' in 2023 that is rooted in science and resonates with zillennial consumers.

With the new key visual launched, I was responsible for scaling the visual system to other applications such as web banners and other digital assets.


The brand refresh strengthened brand equity and helped to build mental availability in the minds of consumers, making Neutrogena a more recognizable brand.
