BRAND & IDENTITY

Distinctive brand assets for Neutrogena®

YEAR – 2023
ROLE – Brand Experience Designer @ Kenvue
STATUS – Launched in 2023, new phase on-going.

Neutrogena® is the #1 dermatologist recommended brand in the US and has been a longtime consumer favourite. However, the brand has been losing relevance among young consumers. In order to future-proof the branding system, the team launched a new point of difference, 'Boost Your Skin's Vitals' in 2023 that is rooted in science and resonates with zillennial consumers.
