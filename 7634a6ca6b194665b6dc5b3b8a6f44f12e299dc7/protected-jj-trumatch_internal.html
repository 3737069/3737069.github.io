UI/UX

TRUMATCH™ personalized solutions

YEAR – 2021, 4 months
ROLE – Digital Experience Designer @ J&J Design
STATUS – Launched in 2021

The goal of DePuy Synthes China is to increase the TomoFix patient-specific instrumentation (PSI) market. TomoFix is a 3D printed cutting guide used in high tibial osteotomy (HTO) that reduces surgery time and improves patient outcome. One of their strategies is to digitalize the end-to-end service of PSI products to better serve Chinese surgeons and targeted patients. The business team reached out to J&J Design to answer this question:

How might we design an effective digital platform for orthopaedic surgeons to order and manage their TomoFix cases so that surgeons have more time in caring for their patients?

Working in a cross-functional team, I was mainly responsible for creating user flows and wireframes for key features of the surgeon platform, as well as collecting feedback and iterating. The result is a shortened lead time from 10 days to 5 days, and the product is projected to twice the market size from 2022 to 2023 (data provided by business team).

Immersion

A 2-month contextual research had been conducted by an external service design agency; gaining first-hand perspectives of surgeons, CT physicians, local distributors, and internal J&J sales and design engineers. The report helped to define the problem and provided actionable insights for us to kick-start designing the platform.

The graph below shows where the most pain points are met across a surgeons' journey, the business team prioritized the following features to be built for MVP 1:

  1. Home dashboard
  2. Case intake form
  3. Design review and approval
  4. Case management

Mapping out task flow

The design team worked closely with the development team to create the surgeon task flows.

1. Home dashboard

The surgeons' mindset when they enter the platform is rarely to browse or explore but to get things done efficiently. To facilitate their workflow, the primary components of the dashboard are:

  • Create new case button
  • Cases pending action table
  • On-going cases
  • Learning resources

2. Case intake form

Submitting the intake form used to be a tedious and manual process, the digitalized form provides a more seamless and secure experience.

3. Design review and approval

Once the engineers have completed the cutting guide design specifications, it would be submitted for the surgeon to review and approve.

4. Case management

A record of on-going and past cases for reference. Users can easily filter and search for cases.

Reflections

Working on TRUMATCH™ has been really gratifying, the platform serves thousands of healthcare providers. It was my first time working on a project with the China team, it was challenging and fast-paced but I learned that the key was having clear communication channels to align with stakeholders efficiently. Overall, it was a really successful MVP launch and I'm excited to see the next version of the platform and its feature improvements.
